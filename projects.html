Nature for Wellbeing

We will be running our project in collaboration with Bristol Future Parks across two locations. Our project's aim is to benefit the local community by facilitating connection to the natural world through workshops and events and to motivate reciprocal work which benefits people and their environment.

Support Group

A support group that brings together young adults and adults whose parents have substance and/or alcohol misuse. Using our personal experiences, we aim to hold a safe space where people can share emotions, life stories and resources, inspiring each other to overcome difficult circumstances and make positive changes in life. These sessions are run in a natural and local space, encouraging an environmentally-respectful perspective. In partnership with ‘The Door’ and ‘Change, Grow, Live’
